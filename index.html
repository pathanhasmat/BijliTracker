1. Limited Liability Disclaimer

        
BijliTracker Pro is a calculation assistance tool for estimating electricity billing. All calculations are based on meter readings and rate configurations you provide. Final billing accuracy and verification rest entirely with the property owner.

        
BijliTracker, its developers, and associated services are not liable for any billing disputes, calculation discrepancies, tenant disagreements, financial losses, or regulatory penalties arising from use of this application. This tool does not replace your DISCOM's official bill.

      

      

        
2. Data Ownership & Privacy Policy

        
Your data belongs entirely to you. All tenant records, bill histories, and payment ledgers are stored in a centralized Supabase database protected by strict Row-Level Security (RLS) policies. Your records are completely isolated — no other user can access your data.

        
We do not inspect, sell, trade, or share your tenant data, mobile numbers, or any personally identifiable information with third parties. Any analytics are aggregated, non-identifiable usage metrics only. Your data is never used to train AI models.

        
Tenant mobile numbers are stored in your account and processed client-side for WhatsApp dispatch only — never transmitted to AI endpoints.

BijliTracker Pro Premium is billed at ₹49 per month on a rolling auto-renewal cycle. Your subscription activates immediately upon successful payment and remains valid for 30 days.

        
Cancellation: You may cancel at any time through Settings → Account. Cancellation stops future auto-renewal but does not entitle you to a refund for the active billing period. Access continues until expiry.

        
Refunds: No partial refunds for unused days. For genuine technical failures (payment debited, premium not activated), contact support@bijlitracker.app within 7 days with your Razorpay Payment ID. All payments processed through Razorpay, an RBI-certified PCI-DSS compliant gateway.
