Welcome to BijliTracker!
Manage electricity bills for all tenants in one place. Calculate, track, and send WhatsApp invoices instantly.
📊Auto-calculate with slab rates
💬Hindi + English WhatsApp invoices
☁️Rate & premium synced across devices

    
⚙️
First: Set Your Rate
Go to Settings → Rate Mode and enter your ₹/kWh rate — or pick your DISCOM slab preset (MSEDCL, BESCOM, UPPCL).
💡 Rate is saved to your account — same on every device.

    
👤
Add Your Tenants
Go to Tenants tab → Add Tenant. Enter name, mobile number, and room. Mobile is used for WhatsApp invoice delivery.
Free plan: 1 tenant. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited.

    
🎉
You're All Set!
Go to Home tab, select a tenant, enter meter readings, and tap Save Bill. Everything calculates automatically.
Tap Invoice on any unpaid bill to send a WhatsApp message.
